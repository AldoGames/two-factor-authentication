Configure Authenticator App To use authenticator app go through the following steps

1. Download a two-factor authenticator app like Microsoft authenticator app for Android or Ios or Google Authenticator app for Android or Ios

2. Scan the QR Code or enter this key {{ myAngularxQrCode }} into your two-factor authenticator app. Spaces and casing do not matter.

3. Once you scanned the QR Code or input the key above, your two-factor authenticator app will provide you with a unique code. Enter the code in confirmation link below.
